What is this Calculator?

This free online calculator helps traders and businesses calculate profit and loss for currency deals and USDT (Tether) transactions. Whether you're dealing with foreign currencies against USDT or buying/selling USDT in PKR, this tool provides instant calculations with accurate profit/loss analysis.

Use Cases

Currency Trading

Calculate profit/loss when trading foreign currencies against USDT. Input currency amount, USDT rate, and PKR conversion to see your margin.

USDT Buying

Calculate profit when buying USDT. Compare market rate with client rate to determine your profit margin per transaction.

USDT Selling

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the currency deal calculator work?

The calculator converts your currency amount to USDT using the current rate, then converts to PKR. It calculates profit/loss by comparing market rates with your client rate in either PKR or USDT.

What's the difference between USDT Buy and Sell?

When selling USDT, you calculate profit based on the difference between client rate and market rate. When buying USDT, profit is calculated when the market rate is higher than the client rate you're paying.

How are deals saved?

All deals are saved to your browser's local storage for the current day. You can view, track, and delete deals. Data persists until you clear your browser data or manually clear deals.

Can I calculate profit in both PKR and USDT?

Yes! For currency deals, you can select whether your client rate is in PKR or USDT. The calculator will automatically show profit in the corresponding currency unit.

Is this calculator free to use?

Yes, this is a completely free, client-side calculator. No registration, no backend, and all calculations happen in your browser.
