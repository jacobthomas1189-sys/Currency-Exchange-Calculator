What is this Calculator?

This free online calculator helps traders and businesses calculate profit and loss for currency deals and USDT (Tether) transactions. Whether you're dealing with foreign currencies against USDT or buying/selling USDT in PKR, this tool provides instant calculations with accurate profit/loss analysis.

Use Cases

Currency Trading

Calculate profit/loss when trading foreign currencies against USDT. Input currency amount, USDT rate, and PKR conversion to see your margin.

USDT Buying

Calculate profit when buying USDT. Compare market rate with client rate to determine your profit margin per transaction.

USDT Selling

Calculate profit when selling USDT. Track your margins by comparing what you receive versus market rates.

Daily Profit Tracking

Save all your deals and track daily profit totals. View transaction history and manage your trading records.

What is the USDT Ledger?

The USDT Ledger is a simple record-keeping feature inside this calculator that helps you track non-trading USDT movements. Instead of calculating profit or loss, it keeps a clean register of USDT to Receive (others owe you) and USDT to Return (you owe others). It is useful for managing credit, loans, and ongoing settlements without mixing them with trading deals.

USDT Ledger Use Cases

USDT to Receive

Record USDT that others owe you. Add the person name, USDT amount, optional rate, notes, and date so you always know how much USDT you are expecting from each party.

USDT to Return

Track USDT that you need to return to others. Use this to manage short-term loans, client advances, or internal transfers that must be settled later.

Daily Record Keeping

Use the ledger as your daily USDT register. Every new entry updates the running totals for USDT to Receive and USDT to Return so you can quickly see your current exposure.

Mobile-Friendly Swipe Table

The USDT Ledger uses a horizontal swipe table on mobile. Swipe left or right to view the full record while keeping all columns visible, even on smaller screens.
